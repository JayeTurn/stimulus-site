A modest JavaScript framework for the HTML you already have.

Sprinkle your HTML with controller, target, and action attributes:

{%- highlight html -%} {%- include_relative _js/controllers/hello_example.html -%} {%- endhighlight -%}

Write a compatible controller and watch Stimulus bring it to life:

{%- highlight js -%} {%- include_relative _js/controllers/hello_controller.js -%} {%- endhighlight -%}

Instead, it's designed to augment your HTML with just enough behavior to make it shine. Stimulus pairs beautifully with Turbolinks to provide a complete solution for fast, compelling applications with a minimal amount of effort.
